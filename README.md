# AI Platform Strategy

A practical collection of frameworks for thinking about AI platforms, shared capabilities, reusable intelligence layers, and product strategy at scale.

This repository focuses on the product side of AI platform thinking:
- when to build shared layers
- what capabilities should become platformized
- how to sequence platform investments
- how to balance speed vs reuse
- how platform strategy creates product and business leverage

---

## Why this repo exists

As AI products scale, teams often face the same questions:

- Should this capability be built once or separately in each product?
- What should live in the application layer vs the platform layer?
- When do platform investments accelerate product velocity?
- When do they slow teams down?
- How do we justify platform work in business terms?

This repo captures a practical way to think about those decisions.
